How It Works?

  1. Consultation

  2. Place the order

  3. Design & Selection

  4. Execution & Installation

  5. Move in

1. Consultation

Raise an enquiry or call us on +91 7802-80-80-80, our Technical expert will get in touch with you and take all your detailed requirements and provide you the estimation for your requirement.

2. Place the order

Place the order by paying the 10% Advance of project value and proceed for the Design phase.

3. Design & Selection

Our experienced Interior designer will provide you the quality Designs as per your requirements and inputs. You can select your material and colors.

4. Execution & Installation

Project execution will be started in the factory. After that the material will be dispatched to the site as scheduled and the Installation will be done by our expert carpenters

5. Move in

We make sure the project completes on time without any hassles. You can Happily move into your Dream Home.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you charge separately for Design?

No, We do not charge separately for Designs. It's inclusive.

Will you design part of my home?

Of course Yes! We would be happy to design part of your home. It can be only Modular kitchen, Living room or Bedroom. Please contact us, we would be happy to help you!

In case if I cancel my project, will I get a refund?

We are sorry to see you go. If you decided to cancel your project within 7 days after paying the booking amount(10%), No Questions asked, Buildhood will return the full refund. And there will no refund after 7 days.

How is Buildhood payment process?

10% on Booking, 40% on start of Production, 40% on start of Installation, 10% on Project Handover.

How do you assure on-time completion of project?

We will plan and define a schedule and timeline for each stage of the project which helps us to meet the target date and we closely monitor all the stages.

How do you monitor the quality of work?

A dedicated and experienced site engineer/supervisor will be assigned for your project and he/she will be monitoring the progress and perform all the Buildhood Quality checks.

Whom to contact during work?

A dedicated Project manager and Site engineer/supervisor will be assigned to each project. You can contact the site engineer or project Manager for any queries.

Does the client need to followup the work regularly?

Not required, You need not follow up or worry about the work progress. Our team will be taking care of it. You can enjoy our hassle-free process and service.

How does the client monitor the progress of work?

We will be sending the status updates through Email or Whatsapp group. photos and videos will be captured at site.

Do you offer any warranty?

Yes, we provide 1 year service warranty.
